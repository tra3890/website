THE GROSVENOR MUSEUM’S 13th OPEN ART EXHIBITION

The show provides a diverse and stimulating exhibition for the public, vividly demonstrating the great wealth of artistic talent in the area. With 203 exhibits by 162 artists, the exhibition presents a kaleidoscope of colours and styles, a wide range of techniques and materials, and a rich variety of subjects and moods.
